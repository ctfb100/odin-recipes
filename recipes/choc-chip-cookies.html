Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Soft and sweet chocolate cookies made easy

Ingredients

  • 1 cub butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions

  1. Gather ingredients, making sure butter is softened, and eggs are room temp.
  2. Preheat oven 350 degrees F(175C).
  3. Beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar with mixer in large bowl until smooth.
  4. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
  5. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt.
  6. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.
  7. Place spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets.
  8. Bake in preheated oven until edges are nicely browned, about 10 minutes.
  9. Cool on baking sheets briefly before removing to wire rack to cool fully.
  10. Store in airtight container or serve immediately and enjoy.
